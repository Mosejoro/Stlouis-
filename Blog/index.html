Interhouse Sport

Annual interhouse sports competition showcases athletic talent and house spirit...

The annual Interhouse Sports Competition was a spectacular display of athleticism, teamwork, and house spirit. Students competed in various sports including track and field, swimming, basketball, and football, demonstrating both their sporting prowess and exemplary sportsmanship.

This year's event saw several school records broken and new talents emerging. Beyond the competition, the event strengthened our school's community spirit and highlighted the importance of physical education in our curriculum.

Spelling Bee Champions

Celebrating our students' outstanding performance at the regional spelling bee competition...

Our students have once again demonstrated their academic prowess by dominating the Regional Spelling Bee Competition. With intensive preparation and dedication, our team secured both first and second places, setting a new record for our school.

Special congratulations to our winners who will represent our school at the national level. Their achievement reflects our commitment to academic excellence and the support of our dedicated teachers and parents.
