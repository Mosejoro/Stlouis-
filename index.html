Welcome to Saint Louis Bida

At St. Louis Nursery and Primary School Bida, we are Niger State's leading institution for early childhood and primary education. Our world-class curriculum is rooted in Christian values, fostering academic excellence and personal growth in every child. Located in the heart of Bida, we provide comprehensive educational programs that prepare students for future academic success.

Celebrating 30 Years of Excellence at St. Louis Bida
